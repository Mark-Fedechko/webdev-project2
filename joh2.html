What is this?

The Journey of Hope is a trans American cycled that aims to raise money for individuals with disabilities. Annually 50-60 brothers of Pi Kappa Phi get together, raise approximately $600,000 and set off to make an impact. Some of the ways the cyclists make an impact is by meeting disabled indivuals, playing sports, and spreading awareness to the disabled community in America.

Why did I get involved?

  • I wanted to make an impact in a community I understand and a for a non profit that I believe in!
  • I wanted the challenge of biking 75 miles on average every day this summer!
  • I wanted to use the talents given to me for the betterment of the community around me.
